Agnolotti al sugo d'Arrosto

Description

This dish will drive you crazy! The pasta we use, agnolotti, will have the perfect taste when mixed with our roast sauce!

Ingredients

Now the real question is, what do we need in order to make it? Don't worry, I got you!

  • 500g Fresh Agnolotti
  • 400g Roasted Veal
  • 1 Carrot
  • Onion
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1/2 Celery Stem
  • 40g Butter
  • 150ml White Wine
  • Meat Broth (To Taste)
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano (To Taste)
  • Extra Virgin Oil (To Taste)
  • Salt (To Taste)

Do be careful!

This recipe does contain some allergens

Steps

Here we'll guide you throughout the whole process, pay attention!

  1. Peel the carrot.
  2. Clean the celery stalk.
  3. Chop the peeled onion, garlic, peeled carrot, and cleaned celery into small pieces.
  4. In a large saucepan, brown the veal with butter and a drizzle of oil, turning it on all sides to ensure even browning.
  5. Once the meat is well browned, add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery.
  6. Let everything cook for about 6â€“7 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, prepare the meat broth in a separate pot.
  8. Pour the wine into the saucepan and let it evaporate completely.
  9. Lower the heat and add half of the hot broth.
  10. Add salt to taste.
  11. Cover with a lid and cook over medium heat for about 1.5 hours, occasionally adding more hot broth as needed.
  12. Blend the meat and vegetables together until smooth.
  13. Cook the agnolotti in salted boiling water.
  14. Drain the agnolotti, toss them with the blended sauce, and top with grated Parmigiano Reggiano.

There you go, enjoy your meal!
