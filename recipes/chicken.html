Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

We think you might like this one, the chicken isn't always crunchy, however, if you enjoy it crispy, this one will definetely please your mouth!

Ingredients

Now the real question is, what do we need in order to make it? Don't worry, we got you!
  • 4 Pounds of Whole Chicken
  • 1 Cup Buttermilk
  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Paprika Seasoning
  • Salt Seasoning (To Taste)
  • Pepper Seasoning (To Taste)
  • 2 Quarts Vegetable Oil

Do be careful!

This recipe does contain some allergens

Steps

Here we'll guide you throughout the whole process, pay attention!

  1. Cut up the chicken.
  2. OPTIONAL: Skin the chicken.
  3. Put the flour in a plastic bag.
  4. Add paprika, salt and pepper to the plastic bag.
  5. Dip the chicken in buttermilk, a few at a time, then coat them in the plastic bag.
  6. Put the chicken in a tray, cover with a clean towel,let it sit until the flour is of a paste-like consistency.
  7. Fill a large skillet (about 1/2 of it) with vegetable oil, heat until VERY hot.
  8. Put as many chicken pieces as you can, let them brown on both sides.
  9. When browned, reduce the heat, cover the skillet and let it cook for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove the cover, increase the heat and let it cook until crispy.
  11. Drain the chicken with a paper towel.

There you go, enjoy your meal!
