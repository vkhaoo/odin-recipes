Tiramisu Toffee

Description

We offer you one of the best desserts you'll ever try! If you try out our recipe, you'll be amazed!

Ingredients

Now the real question is, what do we need in order to make it? Don't worry, I got you!

  • 10.75 Ounces of Prepared Pound Cake, Thawed and Cut in 9 Slices
  • 3/4 Cup Strong Brewed Coffee
  • 8 Ounces of Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Chocolate Syrup
  • 2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1.4 Ounces Chocolate Covered English Toffee, Chopped

Do be careful!

This recipe does contain some allergens

Steps

Here we'll guide you throughout the whole process, pay attention!

  1. Arrange Cake Slices on bottom of a rectangular 11x7 inch baking dish (cutting cake slices if necessary to fit the bottom of the dish).
  2. Drizzle coffee over cake.
  3. Beat Cream Cheese, Sugar, and Chocolate Syrup, in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
  4. Add heavy cream; beat on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  5. Spread over Cake.
  6. Sprinkle with chocolate-covered toffee candy.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, but no longer than 24 hours, to set dessert and blend flavors.

There you go, enjoy your meal!
